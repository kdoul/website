Hi, I'm Konstantinos. I'm an IoT Cloud Solution Architect at Wienerberger,
where I build cloud infrastructure that connects shopfloor edge systems to
ERP, MES, and data lake platforms, keeping security and cost in check
along the way.

Before that I worked as a DevOps engineer, first at World Data Lab and
then at Flexfin, building infrastructure on AWS and Azure with Terraform
and Kubernetes. Further back I was a software engineer on EU public
sector projects at the University of Piraeus, where I also did my
master's in information systems.

Outside of work I run a small Proxmox homelab in the basement and
occasionally break things on purpose to learn how to fix them.
